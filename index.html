    YESS (Young Entrepreneurs Startup Summit) is a dynamic platform for aspiring entrepreneurs, innovators, and young professionals. This event encourages networking, learning, and idea-sharing by providing participants the chance to interact with experts, attend workshops, and take part in startup pitch competitions. YESS aims to inspire young minds to venture into entrepreneurship, offering resources and mentorship to help bring their ideas to life.   • Malabar Hub Meet

    Malabar Hub Meet (MHM) is a three-day annual flagship event of IEEE Malabar Hub aimed at connecting the vibrant volunteers across the Malabar Hub and cheering them up with technical and cultural exposure. The three-day arch event features top-notch programs including technical workshops, mentorship opportunities, industrial visits, and cultural programs. Attended by students spanning different colleges in the Malabar region, this event facilitates a platform for creating the building blocks of a lifelong and diverse professional network. The event caters to the inquisitive minds of young engineers by allowing them to interact with technical experts and alleviate their concerns about the working world while providing real-time industrial networking. The event also focuses on equipping the delegates with volunteering opportunities and improving their communication skills and confidence level. We promise a platform to converse and think with other enthusiastic minds through MHM

  • LINK CAMP

    LINK Camp is an exclusive event designed for IEEE Student Branch representatives, focusing on enhancing leadership, organizational, and communication skills. Participants engage in training sessions and networking activities aimed at boosting their capability to manage and lead student branches effectively. The camp encourages collaboration among branches, fostering growth and development within the IEEE community. It also serves as a platform to exchange ideas, strategies , and best practices, ensuring that student leaders are equipped to take on future challenges.
